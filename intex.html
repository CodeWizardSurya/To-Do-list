Eleanor & Park

Rainbow Rowell

Eleanor & Park" is a heartfelt and poignant novel that follows the unlikely romance between two misfit teenagers, Eleanor and Park, set in 1986. Eleanor is a new student at Park's high school, and she stands out with her eccentric style and troubled home life. Park, on the other hand, is a quiet and introspective boy who loves comic books and music. Despite their differences, they form a connection through their shared love of music and comics, gradually falling for each other as they bond over mixtapes and shared moments on the bus ride to and from school. However, their budding romance faces numerous obstacles, including bullying, family dysfunction, and societal prejudices. As their relationship deepens, Eleanor and Park must confront the challenges of first love and the uncertainty of whether their feelings are reciprocated. "Eleanor & Park" is a beautifully written story about the complexities of teenage love, friendship, and self-discovery, capturing the bittersweet experience of longing for someone who may never love you back in the same way.
